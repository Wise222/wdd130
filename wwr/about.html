White Water Rafting Adventures

At White Water Rafting Adventures, we create unforgettable experiences on some of the most spectacular rivers in the region. Our certified guides ensure your safety while delivering the thrill of a lifetime. Whether you're a first-timer or an experienced rafter, we have the perfect adventure waiting for you!

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1995 by outdoor enthusiasts Mark and Sarah Johnson, our company began with just two rafts and a shared passion for river adventures. Starting on the gentle currents of the Snake River, we quickly gained a reputation for exceptional safety standards and unforgettable customer experiences. Those early days taught us the importance of quality equipment and knowledgeable guides.

Growing Strong

Over the past three decades, we've expanded to offer adventures on five different rivers, serving over 50,000 satisfied customers. Our commitment to environmental stewardship and community involvement has made us a trusted name in outdoor recreation. We continuously invest in the latest safety equipment and guide training while maintaining the personal touch that made us successful from the beginning.
