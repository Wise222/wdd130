Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1995, our company started with just two rafts and a dream. We believed that everyone should experience the thrill of white water rafting in a safe, professional environment. Our founders, John and Sarah Rivers, spent their first season guiding small groups down the local rapids, sharing their passion for adventure and nature.

Growing Strong

Today, we operate across multiple rivers with state-of-the-art equipment and certified guides. Our commitment to safety and customer satisfaction has made us the leading rafting company in the region. We continue to innovate while staying true to our roots - providing unforgettable adventures on the water.
