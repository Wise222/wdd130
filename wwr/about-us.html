White Water Rafting Company

Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable white water rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe in connecting people with nature through thrilling water adventures.

History

Founded in 1995, White Water Rafting Company began as a small family operation with just two rafts. Over the years, we've grown to become one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region, serving thousands of satisfied customers annually while maintaining our commitment to safety and environmental stewardship.
